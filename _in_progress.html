About Us

Andrew Roberts
Daniela Cárdenas

It was a cold and snowy Saturday night in the middle of December. The snow wasn’t going to stop Andrew from an opportunity to socialize, and it certainly wasn’t going to stop him from riding his bike. So, he heads over to his friend LA’s housewarming party.

Meanwhile, Daniela was getting sleepy, and too discouraged to go out to her friends’ Gomez and Nicole’s housewarming party on account of the weather. The roads hadn’t been plowed yet for her to easily ride her bike without slipping all over the place. Begrudgingly, she decided to take the bus. Finally arriving, she walked up onto the porch and saws a black Surly Cross-check single-speed bike locked up on the porch.

Did Camilo ride here? No, he has a geared Cross-check. How the heck could anyone have biked here?

She shrugged off the thought and went on up to the party. “I’m here!” she said, shaking off the snow as she greeted her crew. Andrew looked over, and took notice of this new face that made quite the entrance. He’s struck! He carried on with his conversation with a new friend, the very same Camilo, over near the beverage fridge, but he can’t help looking over at this new lady every minute or so.

Daniela surveyed the room and sees Camilo chatting with someone she has never seen before, but is immediately intrigued by his sharp yet athletic appeal and sweet demeanor. She walked over and interrupts their conversation:

“So, I saw your Surly out there on the porch,” she said to Camilo.

“Actually, that's my bike!” said Andrew. Her trick worked!

A crazy cyclist that’s crazier than me!

“Man, how the heck did you bike here? I only live a mile and half away but I couldn’t stay upright on my bike since the roads haven’t been plowed yet,” she said.

Andrew shrugged off his ride into the party, and the conversation started to flow. It turned out they both shared an affinity for bicycles, and talked mostly about that off and on for much of the night.

The snow showed no signs of letting up as the night drew on, and the party was likewise in no danger of surrendering. Throughout the night, Daniela tried to not read too much into her compatibility with Andrew. She’d just ended a relationship recently, and wasn’t sure she was ready for anything. Nevertheless, her friends kept telling her throughout the night that she has an admirer. Daniela did her best to shrug it off, but the tension was palpable.

The party eventually died down and everyone decided they are too snowed in to leave. Gomez/LA and Nicole set up air mattresses in the living room, and everyone eventually hunkered down for the night. The next morning, Sonia made the most delicious coconut pancakes for brunch, and the group slowly came out of their post-party slumber.

It turns out Daniela, Andrew, Camilo and Sarah all live within close walking distance of each other, so they decided to walk home together. Before they all say their goodbyes, Andrew asked for Daniela’s phone number. She was excited to give it, but then got distracted by other goodbyes and left Andrew hanging, awkwardly.

“So, do you NOT want to give me your number?” asked Andrew.

“Oh, sorry! I got distracted, Yes! Here it is…”

They made plans to get together and talk more about bike things, a good excuse to see each other again. They agree to meet for drinks the following evening, Monday night, and the rest, as they say, was history.

They met on saturday night, had post-party brunch Sunday morning, met up for drinks Monday night, met up again Wednesday night for Whiskey sledding, and then did not have almost a single day apart for the next year—it’s no wonder they decided to tie the knot!
